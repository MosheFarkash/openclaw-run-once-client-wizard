# Business Templates

Use these templates to speed up creative strategy by business type. Pick the closest match and adapt to the user's actual offer.

## Courses / Workshops / Webinars

Best angles:
- Transformation: before/after knowledge gap.
- Mechanism: the new way to get the result.
- Authority: why this teacher/method is credible.
- Speed to clarity: learn the process without months of trial and error.
- Mistake avoidance: what most people do wrong.

Strong hooks:
- “Stop trying to [bad behavior]. Do this instead.”
- “The system behind [desired result].”
- “Most people fail at [topic] because they skip this step.”
- “Build [asset/result] live in one workshop.”

Creative notes:
- Show framework, checklist, screen, map, or before/after workflow.
- Avoid generic “join now” visuals.
- Make the outcome tangible.

## Premium Services / Consulting / Agencies

Best angles:
- Diagnosis: identify the hidden problem.
- Authority: expert process, not generic service.
- ROI/value: cost of doing nothing.
- Exclusivity: limited-fit, not for everyone.
- Proof: client outcome or case logic.

Strong hooks:
- “Your [system] is not broken. Your [specific bottleneck] is.”
- “Before you spend more on ads, fix this.”
- “For businesses doing [specific stage], not beginners.”

Creative notes:
- Premium, clean, low-clutter design.
- Use specificity instead of hype.
- Qualify the audience clearly.

## Ecommerce / Physical Products

Best angles:
- Product demonstration.
- Before/after use case.
- Pain relief / convenience.
- Social proof.
- Offer stack: bundle, discount, guarantee.

Strong hooks:
- “The [product] that fixes [specific problem].”
- “Before you buy another [category], check this.”
- “Designed for people who hate [pain].”

Creative notes:
- Product must be visually dominant.
- Use high contrast, clear benefit text, and one CTA.
- Show scale, use, texture, or result.

## SaaS / Software / AI Tools

Best angles:
- Workflow replacement.
- Time saving.
- Automation.
- Cost of manual work.
- Before/after dashboard.

Strong hooks:
- “Turn [manual process] into [automated outcome].”
- “Your team is still doing this manually?”
- “From [chaos] to [system] in [timeframe].”

Creative notes:
- Show UI, flow diagrams, automation arrows, or dashboard outcomes.
- Avoid abstract robot/AI clichés unless intentionally branded.

## Clinics / Treatments / Wellness

Best angles:
- Pain/problem awareness.
- Safe expert care.
- Before/after confidence.
- Trust and certification.
- Natural daily-life outcome.

Strong hooks:
- “If you feel [symptom], this may be why.”
- “A calmer way to deal with [problem].”
- “Not another generic treatment for [pain].”

Creative notes:
- Keep visuals tasteful and compliant.
- Avoid exaggerated medical claims.
- Use warm, credible, low-pressure design.

## Coaches / Mentors / Personal Brands

Best angles:
- Belief shift.
- Personal method.
- Mistake/lesson.
- Identity transformation.
- Direct invitation.

Strong hooks:
- “The advice I wish I had before [journey].”
- “You don't need more motivation. You need [mechanism].”
- “The real reason [audience] gets stuck.”

Creative notes:
- Face/personality can be central.
- Use strong quote-style hooks.
- Make the point feel personal and specific.
